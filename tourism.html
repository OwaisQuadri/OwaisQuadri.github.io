Tourism in Dubai

There are many reasons that Dubai is one of the most visited cities in the world. There are 3 types of tourist attractions; Hotels/Malls, Monuments/Parks and Museums. Dubai has all of these:

  • The Burj Khalifa Hotel : The tallest building on earth.
  • Burj Al-Arab: The third tallest pure hotel on earth.
  • Atlantis, the Palm: Hotel
  • Dubai Frame: Museum
  • Quranic Park: Monument/Park/Museum

The top two reasons to come to dubai are the record breaking buildings: The tallest building on earth and the tallest standing frame on earth.

Burj Khalifa

The Burj Khalifa (shown above) was originally named, Burj Dubai , meaning "Dubai Tower". It was renamed as a tribute to Sheik Khalifa bin Zayed al-Nahayan, head of the United Arab Emirates and ruler of Abu Dhabi. The construction of the building took from 2004 to 2009 (5 years). and it is still standing above all else as the tallest building on planet earth. This is the main reason that people visit this city. if you dont go anywhere else in Dubai, you must go to the Burj Khalifa and bask in all its glory.

Dubai Frame

The Dubai frame is known as the tallest (150m) standing frame in the world. It is also a museum about the history of the city of Dubai. The museum/artifact is also enclosed in a park so there is more for a tourist to explore. once you reach the top of the museum, on one side, through the window is a beautiful view of New Dubai. The other side is a stunning view of Old Dubai. on the way up, there is a museum of Old Dubai. On the way down is a museum of New Dubai. This becomes a well rounded learning experience about history and culture.
